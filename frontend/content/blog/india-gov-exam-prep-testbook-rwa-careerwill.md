In India, government jobs (Sarkari Naukri) remain one of the most sought-after career paths. The competition is fierce, but in 2026, a new wave of specialized EdTech apps is providing high-quality coaching to every corner of the country.

## The Big Three of Exam Prep

1. **Testbook**: Known for its massive library of mock tests and previous year papers. If you are preparing for **SSC GD** or **RTO exam**, **Testbook** is your best friend.
2. **Rojgar with Ankit (RWA)**: A cult favorite among aspirants in Hindi-speaking regions. **RWA** provides structured video courses and daily quizzes that focus on the core syllabus of state and central exams.
3. **Careerwill**: Founded by top educators, **Careerwill** offers affordable coaching for **UPSC**, **SSC**, and **Banking** exams.

## Specialized Coaching: SSC and UPSC

For those aiming for the highest offices, **Vision IAS** and **Drishti IAS** provide specialized content for **UPSC** preparation. For **SSC** aspirants, **Parmar SSC** and **Adda247** are the go-to choices for subject-specific mastery.

## Why Digital Prep is Winning

Access to **NCERT books and solutions** online has leveled the playing field. Aspirants can now use an **attendance tracker** app to manage their study hours and a **language learning** app like **Hello English** to improve their communication skills for interviews.

## Tools for Daily Learning

- **Daily Hunt** & **Way2News**: For current affairs updates.
- **Adda247**: For comprehensive quiz modules.
- **Selection Way**: For tracking recruitment notifications.

Whether you are using the **PW app** (Physics Wallah) for foundation or **Exampur app** for revision, the 2026 Indian EdTech ecosystem ensures that no student is left behind due to their location.
