## The parent stack in practice

**SIS** for truth (grades, attendance).  
**Comms** for logistics.  
**Learning** for growth—**Khan Academy**, **IXL**, **Lexia**-style literacy tools, **language learning** apps.  

## AI that respects the SIS boundary

AI should summarize *how to help*, not falsify attendance. Use **NotebookLM** on newsletters and syllabi; use **Slate Up** to rehearse hard units with your learner after you read the real assignment in **Google Classroom** / **Canvas**.
