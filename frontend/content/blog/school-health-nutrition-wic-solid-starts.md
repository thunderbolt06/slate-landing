A hungry child cannot learn. This simple truth has led to a surge in health and nutrition apps designed for parents and schools. In 2026, technology is helping families manage everything from government benefits to infant nutrition.

## Navigating Nutrition: WIC and Solid Starts

- **WIC App**: The Special Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children (**WIC**) has a modernized app that helps families track their benefits, find approved stores, and access nutrition education on the go.
- **Solid Starts**: The go-to guide for introducing solids to babies. In 2026, its AI-powered "Safety Checker" identifies potential allergens and choking hazards based on a photo of the food.

## Managing School Life: My School Bucks

For school-aged children, **My School Bucks** is the essential tool for managing cafeteria accounts. Parents can pay for meals, check menus, and set spending limits, ensuring their children always have access to healthy school lunches. 

## The Parent's Command Center

Using a **parent app** like **ParentSquare** or **ClassDojo**, parents can also stay informed about school health policies and vaccination requirements. In many districts, **Navigate 360** and **Raptor Alert** are used to manage school safety and wellness check-ins.

## Why it Matters for Education

Health and learning are inextricably linked. By using apps like **Solid Starts** for early growth and **My School Bucks** for the school years, parents can ensure their children have the nutritional foundation they need to succeed in the **google classroom**.
