The paper planner is dead. In 2026, a student’s entire academic life is managed through digital portals. Whether you are checking your GPA or reporting an absence, these student information systems (SIS) are your command center.

## The Big Three Portals

1. **Infinite Campus**: A comprehensive portal used by thousands of districts. The **Infinite Campus** app allows students to see their schedules, grades, and assignments in a clean, mobile-optimized interface.
2. **PowerSchool**: Perhaps the most well-known SIS. Its **PowerSchool Unified Classroom** integrates with tools like **Schoology** and **Microsoft Teams**, making it a one-stop-shop for digital learning.
3. **Skyward**: Popular for its "Family Access" features, **Skyward** makes it easy for parents and students to stay on the same page regarding attendance and upcoming tests.

## Mobile Mastery: StudentVUE and ParentVUE

Many districts use the **Synergy** platform, which is accessed through **StudentVUE** and **ParentVUE**. These apps provide real-time push notifications for grade changes, helping students catch issues before they become problems.

## Integrating with the Classroom

These portals don't exist in a vacuum. They often sync with your **LMS** (Learning Management System) like **Canvas Student** or **Google Classroom**. In 2026, "Single Sign-On" (SSO) tools like **Clever** and **ClassLink** make it easy to jump between your grades in **PowerSchool** and your assignments in **Canvas**.

## Pro-Tips for Students:
- Enable notifications for "Grade Posted".
- Use the **attendance tracker** feature to ensure your record is accurate.
- Check your **student square** for school-wide announcements.
