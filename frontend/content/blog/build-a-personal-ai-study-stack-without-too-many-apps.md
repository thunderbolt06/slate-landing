## The lean five

1. **Source of truth** — whatever your institution mandates (**LMS** + **SIS** portal).  
2. **Drills** — one memory app (**Quizlet** / **Anki**) + one subject drill (**IXL** / **Khan** / language app).  
3. **Grounded research** — **NotebookLM** when you own the PDFs.  
4. **Structured teaching** — **Slate Up** when you need slides + narration + peer-like AI characters.  
5. **Wellbeing guardrail** — turn off notifications except **Remind** / **Parent Square**-class essentials.
